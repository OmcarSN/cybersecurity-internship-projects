This Was a Phishing Simulation

No data was collected. This page is part of a cybersecurity awareness exercise.

You were about to submit your email, password, and card details to a fake page. In a real attack, that information would go directly to an attacker. This simulation was designed to show how convincing phishing pages can look.

Warning signs on this page
Fake domain in the email link: The email that brought you here came from a domain like "securebank-verify.com" — not the real bank's website. Always check the full URL before entering anything.
24-hour countdown pressure: Real banks do not give you a countdown timer and threaten to suspend your account. This urgency is designed to make you act without thinking.
Asking for your current password: No legitimate service asks for your existing password in a verification form. They send a reset link to your email.
Requesting card details: A login page has no reason to ask for your card number. This is a common tactic to collect financial information.
What to do if you see a page like this Close the tab immediately. Do not fill in any fields. Go to the official website by typing the address directly in your browser. Report the phishing email to your IT team or email provider.
